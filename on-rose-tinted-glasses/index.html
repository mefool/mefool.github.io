on rose-tinted glasses

and hypomania filtering of normal experiences.

most things aren't even that bad. Taking an optimistic point of view, helps on taking chances and living life to its fullest, if that optimistic outlook can be kept. After things go down the cludgie/khazi/shitter/toilet, being extra critical on those things that might have looked great, imposes a abort any possible positive takeways from that thing.

just keep at it. Somethings are better than others, but first they have to be. Doing it and taking chances, thus must be a way to do it. I don't do it that much, but I should get better at it. If the path rellies on a beyond-what-is-expected by you and others, you should consider it a path of unknown expected value until you find/estimate the average value of possible returns.

value ideas and hopes. That's it, it might be all that you have. It might all seem absurd, but that's the way it is. creativity and a non-overly-critical outlook must be prized, knowing life isn't results and titles.
